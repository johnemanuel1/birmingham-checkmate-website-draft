Our approach

Learning That Meets Every Child Where They Are

We combine short lessons, puzzles, friendly games, individual guidance and positive competition. Children progress at their own pace while developing strong foundations.

1

Beginners

Learn the rules, basic tactics, checkmates and confident decision-making through fun, structured activities.

2

Improvers

Strengthen calculation, strategy, pattern recognition and tournament skills using the Chess Steps curriculum.

3

Competitive Players

Prepare for school, county, regional and national events through advanced coaching and game analysis.

More than chess

Skills That Reach Beyond The Board

Children practise concentration, patience, problem-solving and calm decision-making every time they play. They also learn to reflect on mistakes, recover after setbacks and respect an opponent.
